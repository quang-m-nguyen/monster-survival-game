Monster Survival Game

Survive against monsters coming from all directions!

Play Game

How to Play

  • Movement: Use arrow keys or WASD to move
  • Shooting: Automatic! Your character shoots in the direction you're facing
  • Toggle Auto-fire: Press F to toggle automatic shooting on/off
  • Manual Shooting: Press spacebar to shoot manually (even with auto-fire on)
  • Goal: Survive as long as possible and level up
  • Leveling: Kill monsters to level up and choose weapon upgrades
  • Upgrades: Press 1, 2, or 3 to select an upgrade when leveling up
